# -CHOCOLATE-SALES-E-COMMERCE-DASHBOARD
Chocolate Sales E-Commerce Dashboard (Power BI)
1. Executive Summary (Top-Level Takeaway)

The Chocolate Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance and shipment activity for the period 2023–2024. The business generated ₹44.69 million in total sales, with stronger performance in 2023 compared to 2024. While order delivery performance remains strong, the decline in 2024 sales indicates a need for targeted growth strategies and better product-level optimization.

2. Objective / Business Question

The primary objective of this dashboard is to:

Monitor overall chocolate sales performance

Identify year-wise sales trends

Evaluate product and salesperson contribution

Analyze order fulfillment status

Support data-driven decision-making using Power BI

3. Key Metrics / KPIs

The dashboard focuses on the following KPIs:

Total Sales Amount

Total Boxes Sold

Average Order Value (AOV)

Total Shipments

Year-wise Sales Trend

Sales by Product (PID)

Sales by Salesperson (SPID)

Order Status Distribution

4. Insights & Findings
Insight 1 – Sales Trend Decline

What we found:
Sales decreased from ₹25.74M in 2023 to ₹18.95M in 2024.

Why it matters:
This decline indicates slowing demand or increased competition.

Recommended action:
Launch promotional campaigns and seasonal discounts to revive sales.

Insight 2 – Average Order Value

What we found:
The average order value is ₹5,654, indicating medium-sized purchases.

Why it matters:
Customers are consistent but not increasing purchase size.

Recommended action:
Introduce bundled offers and volume discounts to increase AOV.

Insight 3 – Order Fulfillment Performance

What we found:
The majority of orders are marked as Delivered.

Why it matters:
High delivery success improves customer trust and retention.

Recommended action:
Maintain current logistics efficiency and delivery standards.

Insight 4 – Revenue Concentration

What we found:
A limited number of products and salespersons contribute a large share of revenue.

Why it matters:
High dependency increases business risk.

Recommended action:
Improve performance of low-selling products and train underperforming sales staff.

5. Supporting Visuals

The insights are supported using the following visuals in the dashboard:

KPI Cards for quick performance overview

Line Chart for year-wise sales trend

Bar Chart for product-wise sales

Column Chart for salesperson-wise sales

Donut Chart for order status distribution

Table for detailed shipment-level data

All visuals are interactive, clean, and clearly labeled.

6. Limitations

No customer demographic or regional information

No profit or cost data available

Analysis limited to shipment-level sales data

These limitations restrict deeper profitability and customer behavior insights.

7. Recommendations / Next Steps

Implement targeted marketing strategies to recover declining sales

Increase average order value through bundle and bulk pricing

Reduce dependency on a few high-performing products

Enhance future datasets with customer and regional details

8. Conclusion

The Chocolate Sales Power BI Dashboard successfully transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights. It highlights strong operational performance while identifying declining sales trends that require strategic action. The dashboard enables stakeholders to make informed decisions using interactive and visually clear analytics.
